About Simple Learning

So you're probably thinking what more there is to know about a basic tutoring service created up by some guy. Well, this program was created primarily to help the struggling student excel in their STEM courses by relying on a tool more powerful than memorization.

When quizzes, exams, midterms, and finals come around the corner, if you're relying on memorization to pull you through, it'll work sometimes, but as you go on to more specialized courses, dissapointment will be your best friend. Simple learning is centered on helping out students reach the foundational principle of why things are the way they are. It'll definitely require more work than simple memorization, but if you approach whatever principle you're learning in this fashion, you'll find yourself succeeding in those harder courses.
